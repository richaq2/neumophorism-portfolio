Project detail

Reels Reader is a cutting-edge platform that fetches audio, converts it into text, and presents the content in a clear paragraph format, capturing exactly what was said. It seamlessly transcribes the spoken words into written form and also offers a summarized version using OpenAI's technology. This feature provides users with both a full transcription and a concise summary, making it easy to digest and share information. Ideal for content creators, educators, and professionals, Reels Reader turns audio into readable and summarized content quickly and efficiently.

Technique and design combined.

Tech used : HTML, CSS, Django, Javascript

The project involved designing a multi-page website, each with distinct requirements and functionalities. The complexity of the site is reflected in its diverse UI designs.
